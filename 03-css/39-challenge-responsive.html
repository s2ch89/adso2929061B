STORY

Take on your life's work - testing a unique implant that allows you to achieve immortality - and forge your legend in the vast open world of Night City, where your decisions will shape the story and the people around you. Take on all sorts of quests to thrive from emerging merc to cyberpunk legend, while uncovering the mysteries surrounding the priceless implant that everyone wants to get their hands on.

PLAYABILITY

Invest in a variety of skills and perks to form a play style that suits your character. Use weapons, hacking skills and body implants that can be leveled up to become the best mercenary in town. Engage in clean combat, take out your enemies from afar or stealthily infiltrate heavily guarded locations.
